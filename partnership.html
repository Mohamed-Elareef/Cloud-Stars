Our Partnership Process: A Step-by-Step Guide

1

Initial Contact & Assessment

The process begins with a detailed consultation to understand your property and your partnership goals. We'll discuss your expectations, the property's unique features, and answer any questions you may have about our services.

2

Property Evaluation & Documentation

Our team will conduct a thorough on-site evaluation and professional photography session. We'll also help you with all the necessary legal documentation, including proof of ownership, and guide you through the process of securing appropriate insurance to protect your property and its contents.

3

Formal Agreement & Onboarding

Once the evaluation is complete, we'll present you with a tailored partnership proposal, outlining the financial details and a clear scope of work. Upon agreement, we will sign the contract, and you'll hand over keys and access to our team. We'll also finalize a detailed inventory list and perform a final check of the property's condition.

4

Launch & Ongoing Management

With all documentation and preparations in place, your property is ready to be launched on our platform and our network of booking channels. From this point, we assume full responsibility for all marketing, guest communication, booking management, and maintenance, ensuring your property is cared for and generating income.

Choosing Your Partnership Model

Subleasing

In this model, Cloud Stars leases your property on a long-term contract and takes on all responsibility for subleasing to our guests. You get a guaranteed income with zero risk, regardless of occupancy. This is the ideal option for owners seeking a guaranteed, fixed income with zero risk, as we assume full responsibility for all operational and financial aspects.

  • Guaranteed, Fixed Income
  • Zero Risk, Full Responsibility
  • Ideal for hands-off owners seeking stability

Management Only

In this model, you remain the landlord and Cloud Stars acts as your dedicated property manager. We handle marketing, booking, maintenance, and all guest communication for a percentage of the revenue. This option gives you full control over your property, while benefiting from our professional management and marketing expertise to maximize your income.

  • Percentage-Based Revenue
  • Full Control Over Your Property
  • Maximum Flexibility for Personal Use

Addressing Your Subleasing Concerns

Protection Against Damage

We take full financial responsibility for any guest-inflicted damage. Our comprehensive insurance policy covers your property and its contents, and we conduct thorough inspections before and after each stay to maintain its condition.

Legal & Liability Shield

Cloud Stars handles all legal aspects of the tenancy, including guest screening, contracts, and handling any disputes. This shields you from direct liability, allowing you to enjoy a fixed income without the legal and operational stress of managing tenants.

You Don't Lose Control

While we take on the daily management, you remain in full control of your property's long-term status. We maintain transparent communication and provide you with regular reports, so you're always informed about your property's performance and condition.

Professional Care & Maintenance

Unlike long-term tenants, our team performs professional cleaning and maintenance after every guest stay. This ensures your property is consistently kept in pristine condition, preserving its value over time.

High-Quality Guest Vetting

We attract and screen high-quality guests from our trusted network and international platforms. We have strict rules to ensure your property is respected and guest behavior is aligned with our standards.
